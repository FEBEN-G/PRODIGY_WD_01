About Us

Kingdom for God Ministry was founded in 2024 by Mr.x, a passionate Christian and dedicated servant of Jesus Christ. Girum’s vision for the ministry is deeply rooted in his love for the Bible, theology, and biblical philosophy. He established this ministry to share the transformative message of the Gospel and to help others grow in their faith through a deeper understanding of God's Word. At Kingdom for God Ministry, we believe that the Bible is not just a book, but the living Word of God. Through insightful teachings, biblical studies, and community engagement, we are committed to helping individuals discover the truth, hope, and guidance found in Scripture. We aim to equip believers with the knowledge and wisdom needed to live a life that reflects God's love, righteousness, and grace.
